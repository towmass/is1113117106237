Cloud Services

A cloud service can be defined as any kind of service made available to users on demand via the Internet from a cloud computing provider's servers as opposed to being provided from a company's own on-premises servers.

Cloud services' purpose is to provide easy, scalable access to applications, resources and services, and are fully managed by a cloud services provider.

Cited from Webopedia.com 2018

Types of cloud: Examples:
Software as a Service (SaaS) Salesforce
Platform as a Service (PaaS) Cloud 9
Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) Amazon
Communication as a Service (CaaS) Gmail
